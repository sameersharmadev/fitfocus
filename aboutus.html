Our vision

At FitFocus, our mission is to help individuals lead healthier, happier lives by providing personalized meal plans. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality nutrition, which is why our meal plans are offered completely free of charge.

Thanks to the generous donations from our community and the support of our trusted business partners, we can continue to provide these valuable resources at no cost.

Our story

FitFocus was born out of a desire to simplify healthy eating. We noticed that many people struggle with finding the right meal plan that suits their busy lives and nutritional needs.

That's why we created a platform where anyone can receive custom diet plans directly to their inbox, for free. And today, we have created a community of over 100,000 people with a goal of making healthy diet a norm.

Milestones

  • 2017: Launch of FitFocus
    FitFocus was launched with the mission to make healthy eating simple and accessible
  • 2018: 1,000 users world-wide
    Reached our first 1,000 users who trusted us to help them with personalized meal plans.
  • 2019: Partnership with nutrition experts
    Partnered with certified nutritionists to ensure our meal plans are balanced and effective.
  • 2020: 100,000+ success stories
    Celebrated over 100,000 users achieving their health and fitness goals.
  • 2022: Raised $50,000 with help of donations
    This funding allows us to enhance our services, and continue making a positive impact
